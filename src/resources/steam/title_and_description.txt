[h1]Mod Description[/h1]
Replaces the original five loading screens with a selection of paintings by [url=https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmund_Leighton]Edmund Leighton[/url].
Inspired by jin's [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=575946499]Art Replacement Mod[/url] for Crusader Kings II.

[h1]Ironman and Achievements[/h1]
This mod is Ironman and Achievement compatible.
